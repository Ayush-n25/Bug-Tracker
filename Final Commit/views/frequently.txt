What is bug tracking?
Bug tracking is the process of recording and managing bugs that occur in software development.

How does collaboration help in bug resolution?
Collaboration allows team members to work together, share information, and collectively resolve bugs more efficiently.

What is project planning?
Project planning involves organizing and scheduling tasks, setting milestones, and tracking progress to ensure successful project completion.
