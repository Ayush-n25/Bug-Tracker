DefectMonitor - The Software Bug Tracking System

The purpose of the project you described, which manages the track record of bugs in the production line and allows users to manage the track record of the entire project, is to provide a comprehensive solution for bug tracking and project management. The primary purpose is to ensure the effective management of bugs that arise in the production line of code. This involves recording and tracking each bug's details, including its description, severity, status, and assigned developer, throughout its lifecycle. The bug tracking component allows for efficient bug resolution, collaboration among team members, and the ability to prioritize and assign tasks. Additionally, the project aims to enable users to manage the track record of the entire project. This involves features such as project planning, task assignment, progress tracking, milestone management, and collaboration. By providing a holistic view of the project's status and progress, this functionality allows stakeholders to effectively oversee and coordinate the development process.





Why we build this?

"Overall, the purpose of the project is to improve software development efficiency and quality by providing a centralized platform for tracking and managing bugs in the production line, as well as facilitating project management activities for the entire development lifecycle."







"Using a bug tracking system helps streamline the software development process by providing a centralized repository for tracking and resolving issues."


"The bug tracking system enhances collaboration among development teams, enabling them to work together to resolve issues promptly."
